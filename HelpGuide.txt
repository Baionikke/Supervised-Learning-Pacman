Training:

    mlagents-learn configs/MLA-training.yaml --seed 2 --run-id RUN_NAME


Training from previous run:

    mlagents-learn configs/MLA-training.yaml --seed 2 --run-id RUN_NAME --init OLD_RUN_NAME


For results replication on a GNU/linux machine: (takes over 3 days on a RTX2060)

    bash training7Mb.sh | tee training7Mb.log

On windows you have to overwrite the Environments with new .exe files that we do not provide.


Results data access:

    tensorboard --logdir results --port 6006
